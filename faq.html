🌿 About Our Herbs

Yes! We proudly offer a wide selection of USDA-certified organic herbs and spices. Each organic product is clearly labeled and sourced from certified organic farms. We also carry several wildcrafted herbs that are naturally grown without pesticides or chemicals.

We source herbs from trusted growers around the world — Egypt, France, Morocco, Turkey, India, the United States, and more. Each product page lists the country of origin. We work exclusively with suppliers who share our commitment to ethical sourcing and sustainable farming practices.

For maximum freshness and potency, store your herbs in a cool, dark, dry place away from direct sunlight and heat. Our products come in resealable pouches designed to protect from light and moisture. Avoid storing near the stove or in humid areas like above the dishwasher.

Most dried herbs retain their flavor and potency for 1–3 years when stored properly. Whole herbs and flowers tend to last longer than cut-and-sifted or powdered varieties. We recommend checking aroma regularly — if it's lost its smell, it's probably time to replace it.

Our herbs are food-grade quality and are commonly used in teas, cooking, and culinary preparations. However, many herbs have traditional medicinal uses and should be researched before use. We always recommend consulting with a healthcare professional before using any herb therapeutically, especially if pregnant, nursing, or taking medications.
