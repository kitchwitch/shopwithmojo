Orders & Payment

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All transactions are processed securely with SSL encryption.

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After that, our fulfillment team begins processing and we can no longer make changes. Please contact us immediately if you need to make a change.

You'll receive an order confirmation email within a few minutes of checkout. If you don't see it, check your spam folder or contact us and we'll verify your order status.

Shipping & Delivery

Standard shipping takes 5–7 business days within the continental US. Expedited options (2–3 day and overnight) are available at checkout. International orders typically arrive within 10–15 business days.

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all US orders over $49. Orders under $49 have a flat rate of $5.95. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on destination and weight.

We ship to Canada, the UK, Australia, and the EU. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. Please note that customs fees and import duties are the responsibility of the buyer.
