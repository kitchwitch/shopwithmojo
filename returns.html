Returns & Exchanges

Not happy? We'll make it right. Here's everything you need to know about our return policy.

Home / Returns
📅

30-Day Window

Return any item within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

📦

Unopened Items

Items must be unopened and in their original packaging to qualify for a refund.

💰

Free Returns

We provide a prepaid return label for all domestic returns. No restocking fees.

How to Return an Item

  1. Contact Us
    Email support@shopwithmojo.com with your order number and reason for return. We'll send you a Return Authorization (RA) number within 24 hours.
  2. Pack Your Return
    Place the item(s) in their original packaging with the RA number written on the outside of the box. Include the original packing slip if available.
  3. Ship It Back
    Use the prepaid return label we provide (domestic orders). Drop it off at any USPS location or schedule a pickup. International returns are at the buyer's expense.
  4. Get Your Refund
    Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 5–7 business days. The credit will appear on your original payment method.

Exchanges

Want a different product instead of a refund? No problem. We can exchange items within the 30-day window. Here's how:

  • Contact us with your order number and the item you'd like instead.
  • If the new item costs more, we'll charge the difference. If it costs less, we'll refund the difference.
  • We'll ship the replacement as soon as we receive your original return.

Damaged or Defective Items

Received a damaged or defective product? We're sorry about that. Please:

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery.
  • Include photos of the damage and your order number.
  • We'll send a free replacement or issue a full refund — your choice. No return shipping needed for damaged items.

What Can't Be Returned

  • Items that have been opened or used (for hygiene and quality reasons).
  • Items returned after the 30-day window.
  • Custom or special-order items.
  • Items purchased from third-party retailers (please return to the original seller).
