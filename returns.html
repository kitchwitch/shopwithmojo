30-Day Return Guarantee

We want you to love every product you receive. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return unused, unopened items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

How to Return an Item

  1. Contact us — Email returns@shopwithmojo.com with your order number and the items you'd like to return. We'll respond within 24 hours with a return authorization and prepaid shipping label (for US orders).
  2. Package the items — Place the unused, unopened items in their original packaging along with the return authorization number. Seal securely.
  3. Ship it back — Attach the prepaid label and drop the package at any USPS location or schedule a pickup. For international returns, please use a tracked shipping service.
  4. Get your refund — Once we receive and inspect the return, your refund will be processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Items must be unused and unopened in their original sealed packaging
  • Return request must be initiated within 30 days of delivery
  • Items must include the return authorization number (provided by our team)
  • Sale and clearance items are eligible for exchange only, not refunds
  • Gift cards and downloadable products are non-refundable

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you received a damaged, defective, or incorrect item, please contact us at support@shopwithmojo.com within 7 days of delivery. Include:

  • Your order number
  • Photos of the damaged or incorrect item
  • A brief description of the issue

We'll send a replacement or issue a full refund — no return shipping required for damaged or incorrect items.

Refund Details

  • Full refund: Unused, unopened items returned within 30 days
  • Original shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error
  • Return shipping is free for US orders (prepaid label provided)
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–7 business days of receiving the return
  • Please allow an additional 3–5 business days for your bank to post the refund

Exchanges

Want a different product instead? Let us know when you contact us about your return. We'll hold the exchange item while your return is in transit, subject to availability. Any price difference will be charged or refunded accordingly.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Opened or used products (for food safety reasons)
  • Items purchased on final sale or clearance
  • Gift cards
  • Items returned after the 30-day window
