Shipping Policy

Fast, reliable shipping so your herbs and spices arrive fresh and ready to use.

Home / Shipping Policy
📦

Standard Shipping

5–7 business days within the continental US. Free on orders over $49. Flat rate of $5.95 for orders under $49.

⚡

Expedited Shipping

2–3 business days for $12.95. Order by 12 PM EST for same-day processing.

🚀

Overnight Shipping

Next business day delivery for $24.95. Order by 12 PM EST, Monday–Thursday.

Domestic Shipping (United States)

All orders ship within 1–2 business days from our fulfillment center. Orders placed on weekends or holidays are processed the next business day.

  • Continental US: Standard (5–7 days), Expedited (2–3 days), or Overnight
  • Alaska & Hawaii: Standard (7–10 days) or Expedited (3–5 days). Some restrictions may apply.
  • PO Boxes & APO/FPO: Available via USPS. Delivery times may vary.

International Shipping

We currently ship to the following regions:

  • Canada: 7–10 business days, from $9.95
  • United Kingdom: 10–14 business days, from $14.95
  • Australia & New Zealand: 10–15 business days, from $16.95
  • European Union: 10–15 business days, from $14.95

Please note: Customs duties, import taxes, and fees are the responsibility of the buyer. Charges vary by country and are not included in our shipping rates.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number. You can also track your order anytime on our Track Order page.

Lost or Damaged Packages

If your package arrives damaged, please contact us within 48 hours with photos. We'll arrange a replacement or full refund at no cost to you.

If your package is marked as delivered but you haven't received it, please contact us within 7 days and we'll work with the carrier to resolve the issue.

Shipping Restrictions

  • We cannot ship to certain countries due to agricultural import regulations.
  • Some products may be restricted from international shipping.
  • We do not ship to freight forwarders without prior arrangement.

Have a question about shipping? Get in touch — we're happy to help.
