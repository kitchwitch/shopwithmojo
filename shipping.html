Shipping Policy

Fast, reliable shipping to get nature's finest to your door.

Home / Shipping Policy
📦

Standard Shipping

5–7 business days. Free on orders over $49 within the US. Flat rate of $5.95 for orders under $49.

⚡

Express Shipping

2–3 business days. Available for $12.95 within the US. Order by 12 PM EST for same-day processing.

🌍

International Shipping

10–21 business days. Rates calculated at checkout based on destination and package weight.

Order Processing

All orders are processed within 1–2 business days (Monday–Friday, excluding holidays). Orders placed after 12 PM EST on Friday will be processed the following Monday. You'll receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information once your order ships.

Domestic Shipping (United States)

  • Free Standard Shipping on orders over $49 (contiguous US only)
  • Standard Shipping: $5.95 flat rate for orders under $49 — 5–7 business days
  • Express Shipping: $12.95 — 2–3 business days
  • We ship to all 50 states, including Alaska and Hawaii (additional transit time may apply)
  • PO Box and APO/FPO addresses are supported via Standard Shipping

International Shipping

  • We ship to most countries worldwide
  • Shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on destination and package weight
  • Delivery typically takes 10–21 business days depending on the destination
  • Customs fees, import duties, and taxes are the responsibility of the buyer and may be charged upon delivery
  • Tracking may be limited once the package leaves the US

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with your tracking number and a direct link to track your package. You can also visit our Track Order page at any time to check your shipment status.

Lost or Damaged Packages

If your package arrives damaged or doesn't arrive at all, please contact us at support@shopwithmojo.com within 7 days of the expected delivery date. We'll work with the carrier to resolve the issue and send a replacement or issue a refund as appropriate.

Shipping Restrictions

We are unable to ship to US territories outside the 50 states at this time. Some international destinations may have restrictions on importing agricultural products. It is the buyer's responsibility to verify that the products are permitted in their country.
