Common Tracking Questions

Tracking numbers can take 24–48 hours to activate after shipping. If it still doesn't work after that, contact our support team and we'll investigate.

Check with neighbors, building management, or your mailbox. If it's still missing within 24 hours of the "delivered" status, reach out to us and we'll help resolve it.

Once a package is in transit, we can't change the address. However, some carriers (UPS, FedEx) allow you to redirect via their website. Contact us and we'll try to help.
